mUzima

The bottom menu system has been used as opposed to the dashboard in previous mUzima software versions. This menu is divided into pages of:

  1. Home
  2. Cohorts
  3. Forms
  4. Reports
  1. Home
    • This is the page that is displayed when a health care provider logs into the mUzima program.
    • This page has a setting to display the list of clients according to the cohort(s) downloaded from the server.
    • Health care provider can filter this list according to the cohort(s) of patients they want.
    • Similarly, a health care provider can search for a patient in the list of patients using their name in the Find Patient field.
    • In addition, this page has a patient registration button in case the healthcare provider needs to register the patient.
    • Incomplete forms are provided in an area on this page to inform the health care provider of the presence of these forms.
    • Similarly, unsynced forms are allocated a section on this page to inform the health care provider of the existence of these forms.
  2. Cohorts
    3. This page is divided into three tabs.
    1. SHOW ALL tab
      2. This tab lists all the patient groups in the DOWNLOADED and AVAILABLE ONLINE tabs.
    2. DOWNLOADED tab
      3. This tab lists the patient groups downloaded into the mUzima App.
    3. AVAILABLE ONLINE tab

      4. The health care provider can download the desired cohort(s) from this tab by selecting the cohort(s) the click on the download icon that comes up at the top right of the screen

  3. Forms
    4. This page is divided into three tabs.
    1. SHOW ALL tab
      2. This tab lists the forms in the DOWNLOADED and AVAILABLE ONLINE tabs.
    2. DOWNLOADED tab
      3. This tab lists the forms downloaded in the mUzima program
    3. AVAILABLE ONLINE tab

      4. The health care provider can download the required form(s) through this tab by selecting the form(s) the click on the download icon that comes up at the top right of the screen

  4. Reports

    5. This page lists health worker performance reports. These reports can be downloaded from the server-side.
